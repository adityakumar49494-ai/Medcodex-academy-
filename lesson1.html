Lesson 1: Introduction to Medical Coding

📘 What is Medical Coding?

Medical coding is the process of converting healthcare diagnoses, procedures, medical services, and equipment into universal medical alphanumeric codes.

These codes are used for billing, insurance claims, and maintaining medical records.


🏥 Why is Medical Coding Important?

  • Ensures accurate billing
  • Helps insurance reimbursement
  • Maintains standardized healthcare data
  • Reduces medical errors

📚 Main Coding Systems

  • ICD-10 – Diagnosis codes
  • CPT – Procedure codes
  • HCPCS – Equipment & services codes
